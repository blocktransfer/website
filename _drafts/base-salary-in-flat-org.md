Idea for base salary modifications for team members with changing life circumstances requiring more financial or just generally increased reliable means of self-funding.

You could exchange # XXX / month to a limit of 2MM & then you continue for a permanent base salary increase of #YYY

Pros

- Team members that need more security can work hard & use performance bonuses to secure a certain high temporary increase in cash without not paying entire pr.

- Decreased turnover among members opting to incr.

- Members can feel like they're getting a piece of the pie when it comes to part of the company & its profits, without requiring equity distribution which ensures that I maintain ownership/growth/company vision until I donate all the shares to Proygon, allows Proygon contributors to fully empower B1 operation

Cons

- Could compound to current significant future liabilities.

- Future value of $ could lead to initial early that I maintain control which could lead me to too much control over the company which could slow down initial growth. This could also prevent me from needing early advisors who could help us make the company more profitable at work of such individuals would significantly shift focus to generation & retaining economic rents.
